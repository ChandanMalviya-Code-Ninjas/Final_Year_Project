# Health-AI Poster

## PROJECT TITLE: HEALTH-AI

---

## 🧠 ABSTRACT
Health AI is a smart system that leverages machine learning and deep learning techniques to analyze medical data and support early diagnosis, personalized recommendations, and preventive health planning. By combining patient symptoms, history, wearable data, and medical knowledge, Health AI provides intelligent insights to improve wellness outcomes and reduce clinical workload.

---

## 🧩 INTRODUCTION
Health care is rapidly transforming with AI-driven tools that help clinicians and individuals make data-informed decisions. Health AI aims to analyze patient symptoms and medical history, predict risks for common conditions (e.g., diabetes, cardiovascular disease), provide personalized lifestyle and medication recommendations, and enable real-time monitoring using wearable device data. This system is designed to be accessible, reliable, and supportive of both users and healthcare providers, enhancing outcomes while reducing time spent on routine diagnostics.

---

## 🧠 ALGORITHMS USED
- **Classification Algorithms**: Decision Trees / Random Forests and Logistic Regression for risk stratification and binary outcomes; Support Vector Machines (SVM) for high-dimensional medical data classification
- **Neural Networks**: MLP / CNN for complex pattern detection in signals, images, or large feature sets
- **Similarity and NLP**: K-Nearest Neighbors (KNN) for similarity-based symptom matching; Natural Language Processing (NLP) for processing user symptom text input
- **Clustering**: K-Means / DBSCAN for identifying patient subgroups or patterns in health metrics

## ✅ CONCLUSION
Health AI transforms raw medical and lifestyle data into actionable insights, empowering users to monitor health proactively and enabling clinicians to deliver more accurate care. By combining proven ML algorithms and real-world health datasets, the system aims to improve early detection, enhance preventative care, and support better long-term outcomes with an accessible, user-centered design.
