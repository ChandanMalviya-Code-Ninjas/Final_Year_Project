## 🎤 Audio Guidelines

### Voice Recording

- **Microphone:** Use quality USB microphone or built-in
- **Environment:** Quiet room, no background noise
- **Pacing:** 150 words per minute (practice timing)
- **Tone:** Professional, enthusiastic, confident

### Background Audio

- **Style:** Subtle, uplifting instrumental
- **Volume:** 20-30% of voice volume
- **Source:** Royalty-free music (Epidemic Sound, YouTube Audio Library)

---

## 🎥 Recording Setup

### Software Options

1. **OBS Studio** (Free, Professional)
2. **Camtasia** (Paid, User-friendly)
3. **Bandicam** (Paid, Lightweight)
4. **Built-in Windows Game Bar** (Free, Basic)

### Recording Settings

- **Resolution:** 1920x1080 (Full HD)
- **Frame Rate:** 30 FPS
- **Format:** MP4
- **Quality:** High (target 20-30MB final size)

### Multi-Track Recording

1. **Track 1:** Voice narration
2. **Track 2:** Background music
3. **Track 3:** System audio (if showing demos)
4. **Track 4:** Sound effects (optional)

---

## ✂️ Editing Process

### Step 1: Record Raw Footage

- Record voice over script first
- Record screen demonstrations separately
- Capture static slides and graphics

### Step 2: Assemble Timeline

- Import all assets into editing software
- Align voice narration with visuals
- Add transitions and effects

### Step 3: Audio Mixing

- Balance voice, music, and sound effects
- Add fade-ins/outs for smooth transitions
- Normalize audio levels

### Step 4: Final Export

- Export at target resolution and bitrate
- Compress to meet 50MB limit
- Test playback on different devices

---

## 📱 Mobile Optimization

### If Recording on Phone

- Use landscape orientation
- Stable tripod or phone stand
- External microphone for better audio
- Good lighting (face towards window)
- Clean background or professional backdrop

### Apps Recommended

- **PowerDirector** (Android/iOS)
- **InShot** (Android/iOS)
- **CapCut** (Android/iOS)
- **iMovie** (iOS only)
