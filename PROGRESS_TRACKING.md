## 🚨 RISKS & MITIGATION

| Risk | Impact | Probability | Mitigation |
|------|--------|-------------|-----------|
| Backend delays | 2-3 weeks | Medium | Hire 2 backend devs, not 1 |
| HIPAA non-compliance | Delay launch | Low | Start audit week 1, not week 9 |
| App store rejection | 2-4 weeks | Low | Beta test with store guidelines |
| Major bug post-launch | User churn | Medium | Heavy load testing week 11 |
| Team member leaves | 1-2 weeks delay | Medium | Cross-training, documentation |
| API rate limits | Crashes | Low | Cache aggressively, monitor usage |
| Database performance | Slow app | Medium | Proper indexing, sharding plan ready |
| Security breach | Catastrophic | Low | Bug bounty, pen testing, SOC2 audit |
